PROMISES:

  • IMPROVING TECHNOLOGY

    We have some amazing technology at our disposal, like Moodle and Daymap. However, they're cluttered, messy, and frustrating to use.

    As prefect, I'll gather and use student feedback to improve them, meaning they will be able to integrate seamlessly into our learning.

  • FUN PROGRAM X!

    Yes, it's possible!

    Let's face it, Program X can get a little boring, especially in senior years. Even though you might not like to admit it, it can be fun and a time grow as a homegroup or year level.

    There are times when everyone's involved in what they're doing, and having a great time. So, why can't that happen more often?

  • BETTER COMMUNICATION

    As students, we should be aware and involved in decisions that affect us.

    I think it should be everyone's right, in a leadership position or not, to contribute to choices concerning them. I'll ensure that everyone and anyone can make suggestions and further help in any decision making process.

  • MORE EVENTS

    Events such as Wellbeing Week, when clubs get opportunities to show off what they're about and recruit some new members, and when the whole school has a chance to get involved, really are great. They should happen more often, even if it's just a couple of clubs getting together in the Rod Pike area.
